melody and rhythm
* are considered two distinct things in music
  + only for practical reasons, I my opinion
* they are the same
  + both are resonance/ferquency, nothing else
  + measured in Hertz or beat, doesn't matter

in music, tunes/melodies tend to have their own inherent rhythm (tempo/frequency)
* yes, you can slow it down or speed it up creatively - at your own peril and the possible demise of the melody
  + eg. Crested Hens
* yes, it's probably true within a range
  + you can play slightly faster or slower and be still valid
* Astracan Cafe
  + listen to the original
  + search Youtube for cover versions
    - none that I have heard gets it right
  medium tempo is the most difficult one to get it right anyway
