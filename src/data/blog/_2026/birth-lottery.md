An innocent game that touches on some deeper stuff. Spin the globe and see where you might land/could have landed. [play here](https://www.givingwhatwecan.org/birth-lottery)

It's so evient we don't even think about it.

We have landed in this life once. None of us was asked where and when we wanted to grow up.

It's not a metter of
* choice
* merit
* decision, good or bad

The things we are given without being part of the giving act in any way:
* our parents and siblings and everything that comes with them
  + language
  + attitudes in life: "culture"
  + inherited traumas
  + inherited solutions
  + mental models of everything
  + opportunities of education
  + opportunities of getting by financially
* our community
